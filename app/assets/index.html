    About Robert G. Raleigh

    I'm Robert G. Raleigh — an agile IT project manager & frontend developer working in Portland, OR with 10+ years of combined project management, team leadership roles, and technology consulting experience since 2004.

    I'll contract directly with your digital agency, or forward-thinking small-to-mid-size business to help your team plan, iterate, development, launch, and maintain your ongoing or upcoming small and large-scale technology projects.

    Throughout my career, I've launched more than 100+ enterprise apps, mobile apps, custom CMS websites, e-commerce stores, and static sites, managing a client roster of 5 – 7 projects, with budgets ranging from $5,000 – $250,000.

    I hold a Bachelor's Degree in English, from Portland State University, with a concentration in professional writing. I've also completed additional Management/Supervisory Development training at Portland Community College in project management and leadership.

    Outside of work, I’m passionate about songwriting,slide blues guitar, and intergenerational learning. Most Sundays, I can be found in the yellow or blue room at Powell’s Books, looking for the next great mystery or short story writer.
